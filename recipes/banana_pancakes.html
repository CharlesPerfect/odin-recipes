Banana Pancakes

What are Banana Pancakes ?

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs,
milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.
It is a type of batter bread.

The first recorded mention of pancakes dates back to ancient Greece and comes from a poet
who described warm pancakes in one of his writings.

Ingredients :

Serving per person

  • 1 egg
  • 1 banana
  • flour
  • baking powder

Steps :

  1. Mash bananas until they turn into a mushy pudding
  2. Add an egg and mix
  3. Add some flour to thicken up the mixture
  4. Add 1/3 tsp of backing powder
  5. Add some flour according to the mixture. Expecting a pancake batter consistency
  6. Add butter to the pan. Scoop in the mixture and cook on medium low heat for 3 to 4 minutes then flip.
    Expected golden brown crust. Then leave on second side for about a minute and take them out
  7. Dish them up
